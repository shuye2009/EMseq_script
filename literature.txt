Summary of Key Themes
Major pathways affected by radiation:

Cell cycle arrest & checkpoint activation
DNA damage response & repair (NER, BER, HR, NHEJ)
Apoptosis & programmed cell death
p53-mediated transcription
Inflammation & immune response
Metabolic reprogramming
Circadian rhythm disruption
Dose-dependent effects:

Low dose: Adaptive responses, specific pathway activation
High dose: Widespread transcriptional changes, cell death pathways
Temporal dynamics:

Early (0-4h): DNA damage response, cell cycle checkpoints
Intermediate (4-24h): Metabolic changes, stress responses
Late (>24h): Tissue remodeling, long-term adaptations
Epigenetic changes:

DNA methylation alterations (global hypomethylation)
Transposable element activation (LINE-1, Alu)
Histone modifications
Non-coding RNA dysregulation
